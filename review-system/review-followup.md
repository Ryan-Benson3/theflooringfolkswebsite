# Follow-Up Template — 7 Days After Job (No Review Yet)

## When to Use
Send this **7 days after the initial review request** if the customer hasn't left a review yet. This is a gentle nudge — not a push.

---

## SMS Follow-Up

```
Hey [Name]! Just checking in — hope you're loving your new [service]. If you get a chance, we'd still really appreciate a quick review: [link] — Ryan
```

**Character count:** ~145

## Important Rules

- **Only send ONE follow-up.** If they don't review after this, let it go. Pestering hurts more than it helps.
- **Never guilt-trip.** Keep it warm and light — it's an invitation, not an obligation.
- **Don't send if they had issues.** If the job had any problems or complaints, skip the follow-up entirely.
- **Timing:** 7 days after the first request, between 10 AM – 5 PM CT
- **If they respond negatively** to the follow-up, apologize and move on. "No problem at all — just wanted to make sure you're happy with everything!"

---

## What NOT to Do

❌ Send more than one follow-up
❌ Say things like "we noticed you haven't reviewed us yet" (feels surveillance-y)
❌ Offer incentives for reviews (against Google's policy)
❌ Make them feel guilty
❌ Send late at night or early morning
