## QR Code Instructions

To make it even easier for customers, include a QR code they can scan with their phone camera:

### How to Create the QR Code

1. **Go to** [https://www.qr-code-generator.com](https://www.qr-code-generator.com) or use [https://goqr.me](https://goqr.me)
2. **Paste your direct Google review link** into the URL field
3. **Download** the QR code as a PNG image
4. **Add it to the email** below the review button with this text:

> *Prefer to scan? Point your phone camera at this code — it'll take you right to the review page!*
>
> **[QR CODE IMAGE HERE]**

### Where to Place It in the Email
- Below the "Leave a Google Review" button/link
- Before the sign-off
- Keep it about 150x150 pixels so it's easy to scan but doesn't overwhelm the email
