# Review Response Templates — The Flooring Folks

## General Rules

- **Respond to every review within 24 hours**
- **Use the customer's name** — it shows you're paying attention
- **Mention the specific service** — reinforces what you do for SEO
- **Keep it genuine** — no copy-paste corporate speak
- **Include location references** when natural (helps local SEO)

## Response Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Respond within 24 hours
- Use the customer's first name
- Mention the specific service they got
- Thank them regardless of rating
- Offer to resolve issues offline (phone/email)
- Keep a warm, neighborly tone
- Reinforce your service area for SEO

### ❌ Don't
- Get defensive or argue
- Make excuses
- Dismiss their experience
- Get into details of a dispute publicly
- Use corporate jargon ("We value your feedback and strive for excellence...")
- Copy-paste the exact same response every time
- Ignore negative reviews (silence looks worse)
- Blame the customer

---

## For Fake or Unfair Reviews

If you receive a review that's clearly fake, from someone who was never a customer, or violates Google's policies:

1. **Don't respond emotionally**
2. **Flag it on Google** — click the three dots next to the review and select "Report review"
3. **Post a calm, professional response:**

> We take all feedback seriously, but we don't have a record of this project in our system. If you were a customer of ours, please reach out to us directly at (262) 327-3989 so we can look into this and make things right.
>
> — Ryan, The Flooring Folks

This shows prospective customers that you're responsive and reasonable, even when reviews may not be legitimate.
