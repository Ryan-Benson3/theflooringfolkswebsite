# Review Request SMS Template — The Flooring Folks

## Primary Template (under 160 characters)

```
Hey [Name]! Thanks for choosing The Flooring Folks for your [service]. Mind leaving us a quick review? [link] — Ryan
```

**Character count:** ~120 (leaves room for longer names/services)

---

## Alternate Versions

### Version 2 — Casual
```
Hi [Name]! Loved working on your [service]. Would mean a lot if you could drop us a quick review: [link] - Ryan at TFF
```

### Version 3 — Direct
```
[Name], your new [service] looks great! Got 30 seconds? Here's our review link: [link] Thanks! — Ryan
```

### Version 4 — Warm
```
Hey [Name]! Hope you're enjoying those new floors. If we earned it, we'd be grateful for a quick review: [link] — Ryan
```

---

## Usage Notes

- **[Name]** — Customer's first name only (keep it friendly)
- **[service]** — Short description: "new floors," "tile work," "LVP install," "hardwood," etc.
- **[link]** — Your direct Google review link (use a URL shortener like bit.ly to save characters)
- **Sign off as Ryan** — Personal touch matters; don't use a generic business name
- **Send within 2 hours** of job completion while the experience is fresh
- **Time of day:** Send between 9 AM – 6 PM CT to be respectful

## URL Shortener Tip

If your Google review link is too long:
1. Go to [bit.ly](https://bit.ly) or [tinyurl.com](https://tinyurl.com)
2. Paste your Google review link
3. Copy the shortened URL
4. Use that in your SMS

**Example with short link:**
```
Hey Sarah! Thanks for choosing The Flooring Folks for your LVP install. Mind leaving us a quick review? https://bit.ly/3xReview — Ryan
```
